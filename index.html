About

As the Director of the Marine Corps Entertainment & Media Liaison Office in Los Angeles, I serve as the primary connection between the entertainment industry and the United States Marine Corps. My mission is to facilitate authentic partnerships that showcase the values, traditions, and stories of Marines while supporting creative projects that honor our service members.

With years of military experience and deep understanding of both the entertainment industry and Marine Corps operations, I help bridge the gap between Hollywood and the military, ensuring accurate representation and meaningful collaborations.

Resources & Partnerships

The Marine Corps Entertainment & Media Liaison Office offers comprehensive support for entertainment projects seeking authentic military representation:

  • Technical military consulting and expertise
  • Access to Marine Corps equipment and locations
  • Coordination with active-duty Marines and veterans
  • Script review and authenticity verification
  • Public affairs and media relations support
  • Educational resources about Marine Corps culture and traditions
